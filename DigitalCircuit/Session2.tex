    {\color{hwSolution}

        In case of 3 Highs and 1 Low, we get:
        \begin{equation*}
            \left\{\begin{array}{lcl}
                5V - R\cdot I_{CC} &<& 0.3V \\
                I_{CC} + 0.4mA \times 2 + 100\mu A \times 3 &<& 8 mA
